How to Play

  1. Don't Google it, the Elf is watching you!
  2. Choose a card from 1 to 25
  3. Each question you answer correct you score one point
  4. Game is over when you answer 10 questions
  5. Be as assertive and quickly as possible to get a good score!
  6. Challenge your friends and have a fun Christmas!
