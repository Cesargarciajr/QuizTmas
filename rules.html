How to Play

  1. Divide your friends in two teams
  2. Select which team starts first and choose one of the card numbers
  3. Answer the Quiz question and if you team get it right in 30 seconds, you scores one point
  4. If your team got the wrong answer, the opponent team has chance to get it right
  5. If the opponent team got it right, they score one point
  6. Whoever picks a "Gift", scores one point
  7. Whoever picks a Santa Klaus, scores two points
  8. Game ends when there is no more cards to pick and the winner is declared by the team that has more points
