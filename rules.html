How to Play

  1. Don't Google it, the Elf is watching you!
  2. Choose a card from 1 to 25
  3. Each question you answer correct you score one point, if not you loose one point
  4. Be careful!! The Grinch is out there! You might find him, if you do, you loose 2 points
  5. But the good craic is, Santa Claus is coming to town! Because you were a good kid, you can get 3 points if you find him
  6. Be as assertive and quickly as possible to get a good score!
  7. Challenge your friends and have a fun Christmas!
